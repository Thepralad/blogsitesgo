Welcome to sNet

sNet is an online directory that connects Salesian students through social networks.

We have opened up sNet for popular consumption at Salesian College.

  • Search for people at your colelge
  • Find out who are in your departments
  • Look up your friends' friends
  • See a visualization of your social network

To get started, click to register. If you have already registered, you can log in using your @salesiancollege.net email ID.
